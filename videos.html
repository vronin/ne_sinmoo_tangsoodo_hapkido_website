Recommended Books

  • Book Hapkido: Traditions, Philosophy, Technique — Marc Tedeschi. A comprehensive encyclopedic reference covering the full breadth of Hapkido techniques.
  • Book Hapkido Bible — Dr. He-Young Kimm. An authoritative text on Hapkido history, philosophy, and technique by a senior master of the art.
  • Book Sinmoo Hapkido Curriculum Handbook — Dr. Sean Bradley (ISBN 978-0-9850865-0-3). Covers White through 4th Dan with structured curriculum guides.
